The Minimalism Game

This challenge will help to freshen up your space and save more of your mental energy for everything else. Some say, a cluttered space equals a cluttered mind. So here’s how the 30 day minimalism challenge works: on day 1 of the challenge you pick one item to declutter by donating it, recycling it, or throwing it away — (whatever makes the most sense for the item you picked) — and on day 2 you declutter 2 items, and on day 3, three items, and so on up until day 30 where you’ll declutter 30 items!

You can declutter something as small as a paperclip or delete a photo from your camera roll, or something as large as a piece of furniture. ANYTHING goes! It can be a little intimidating to declutter so many things each day, because of that we added an option for choosing the number of days for this challenge.
